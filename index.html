Oh, Mother Father God,

Clothe us with the cloak of zeal. Fill my spirit with zealousness for this week’s work, successes, and my ever-present reality. Grant me discernment and wisdom to interpret my ever-present reality that will build/support the beliefs that I with the ever-present reality can share be a positive and harmonious experience. Illuminate my path forward this week revealing strongly my purpose in the moment as to create a better life for myself and all those whom I love. Allowing me to feel completely comfortable as I get to share in experiences where all whom I love are ignited in your spirit just as I am. Grant me great discernment to not pick up any offense.
